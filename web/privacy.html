Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 7, 2026  ·  museaimusician.com

1. Who we are

Muse is operated by Brendan Lynch. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it. Contact: vowringscompany@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data — when you sign in with Google we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. We store these to identify your account.
  • Usage data — we record how many generations you have used and when, to enforce plan limits and reset monthly counts.
  • Prompts — the text prompts you type are sent to the Anthropic API to generate music. We do not permanently store your prompts after generation is complete.
  • Saved files — MIDI and audio files you explicitly save are stored in your library.
  • Payment data — billing is handled entirely by Stripe. We store your Stripe customer ID and subscription status but never see your card number.

3. How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To enforce generation limits and subscription entitlements
  • To process payments through Stripe
  • To generate music using the Anthropic Claude API
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Third-party services

  • Google OAuth — used for sign-in. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — your prompts are sent to the Claude API to generate music. Anthropic's data practices apply to this processing.
  • Stripe — handles all payment processing. Stripe's Privacy Policy applies to payment data.
  • Railway — our hosting provider stores your data on their infrastructure.
  • Cloudflare R2 — stores drum sample packs used for audio rendering.

5. Data retention

We retain your account data while your account is active. Saved library files are kept until you delete them. If you close your account, your data is deleted within 30 days.

6. Your rights

You may request a copy of your data, correction of inaccurate data, or deletion of your account at any time by emailing vowringscompany@gmail.com. EU/EEA users have additional rights under GDPR including the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

7. Cookies

We use a single httpOnly session cookie to keep you signed in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

8. Children

Muse is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes

If we materially change this policy we will notify you by email. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

10. Contact

Privacy questions: vowringscompany@gmail.com
